Course Description:


Maple Grove is a private, 18-hole golf course tucked along the coastline of Northern Michigan. Designed by architect Warren Henderson in 1972, the par 72 course located just outside of Petoskey, Michigan offers a challenging playing distance of 7,296 yards from the champion tees. With three different sets of tees, the course offers the opportunity for an exquisite golf experience for all skill levels. Overall, the beatifully groomed course, along with numerous fine-dining and lodging opportunities in nearby Petoskey offer the perfect opportunity for an unforgettable golfing vacation.
